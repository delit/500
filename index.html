The Game

1 to 500

The challenge is simple: place 10 random numbers in ascending order. One wrong move ends the run.

How far can you go?

How to play

Ten draws from 1 to 500. Tap empty slots to preview the current number, then confirm to lock it. Order must stay strictly increasing from slot 1 through slot 10. Fill all ten to win.

The draw

Each round shows a new random number from 1–500.

Place

Tap an empty row to place the current number. Tap another empty row to move it.

Confirm

Use the Confirm button on that row, or tap the same slot again to lock it in.

Win

Fill all ten slots in valid order to win.
